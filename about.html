About Koko's Coffee

Koko's Coffee was created because Koko was frustrated about the fact that the Indian coffee beans were being brewed using western techniques, and not the traditional ways that have been passed down through generations. Koko decided to take maters into her own hands and started her very own, authentic coffee company.

Growth throughout the years

Starting from a small store in Chandigarh in the year 2019, Koko's coffee has now grown into 7+ stores across the country.
